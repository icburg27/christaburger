AI Productivity Is Too Vague. Show the Burden Removed.

Not vague productivity. Specific relief with receipts.

✦ Part of the series Things I've Learned Experimenting With AI

“AI improves productivity” is true and also not very useful.

Productivity where?

Which burden got lighter?

What changed?

  • Did we reduce rework?
  • Did we prevent something from being forgotten?
  • Did we make decisions cleaner?
  • Did we improve evidence?
  • Did we reduce manual reconstruction?
  • Did we make the next step obvious?

That is what I care about.

Not vague productivity.

Specific relief with receipts.

This is how AI use cases should be explained.

Before: scattered context, manual prep, repeated follow-up, no clean record.

After: summary drafted, risks visible, owner assigned, evidence linked, decision captured.

That is the story.
