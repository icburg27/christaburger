# Brand Readout Addendum — Observations from the Operational Layer

*Added July 2, 2026 by Caleb, following the v2 readout. These are additions from working context the readout didn't fully capture.*

## 1. The title moved — handle it deliberately

Between the readout and implementation, Christa stepped into interim CISO at a SaaS company. Decision recorded: public credential stays **VP of Cybersecurity** until the seat is permanent. When it becomes permanent, the upgrade cascades in this order: LinkedIn headline → site proof bar → media kit → speaker intros. The brand architecture doesn't change; only the proof strengthens. (Note: "CISO" will eventually make "governance expert whose work encompasses AI" land even harder — the title *is* the positioning.)

## 2. Purple is not an accent — it's the anchor

The readout proposed Ink Navy as the authority anchor with Blackberry as occasional depth. Christa's correction: purple is her color. Implemented palette: **Blackberry #34224A as the authority anchor** (hero, nav, headings, CTAs), parchment #F6F1E7 ground, warm gold #C8994C, stone #E7E1D5, rose clay accent. The result keeps the premium field-guide atmosphere while being unmistakably hers. Navy demoted to rare ink-depth accents.

## 3. Proof points the readout underweights

- **@Pretty_Dials** — the watch channel is live and growing (~100 subscribers/week). The readout treats "the watch as talisman" as a styling note; it is already a running platform. When it crosses critical mass it becomes a public proof of the "systems that tell stories" pattern — horology as governance metaphor is content, not just wardrobe.
- **TAGC** — the agent-governance lane on the site is not speculative thought leadership; there is a company forming behind it (certification authority for well-formed AI: SOUL.md, intent architecture, drift detection, succession). Site copy deliberately holds this at "advanced lane" altitude until TAGC is ready for public linkage.
- **2nd Thought** — the Automated Household lane is also product research for an app addressing invisible mental labor. The website's household page doubles as market validation surface. Same discretion: no public linkage yet.
- **CVA board, 14 years** — quiet but potent governance credibility (community-scale, long-duration). Consider adding to About/media kit as one line.
- **Archaeological field work** — already flagged as "field notes/aliveness," but it photographs better than anything else in the brand. The petroglyph/Western-archive imagery should become the site's visual library backbone (replacing stock).

## 4. Voice note

Christa thinks from a felt sense — an itch before articulation. The strongest posts will be the ones that start from a fragment (a watch movement, a panel in the desert, a Tuesday failure at home) and arrive at governance, rather than starting from governance. The field-note lane isn't decoration; it's the engine of originality.
