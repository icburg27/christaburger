# Voice & Messaging Guide — Christa Burger

## Brand essence

Christa Burger builds humane governance systems for complex work: structures that help leaders, households, and enterprises move with wisdom, clarity, accountability, courage, and sustainable care.

**Promise:** I help leaders and households stop carrying invisible work alone and start governing complexity wisely.

**Role:** The translator between executive risk, leadership formation, AI complexity, household care, human behavior, spiritual conviction, and practical operating systems.

## Positioning anchor (use everywhere internally)

Christa Burger is a cybersecurity governance executive and systems architect helping leaders build humane, accountable operating systems for complex work. Her expertise encompasses AI governance, enterprise trust, leadership clarity, and the home-life systems that protect human judgment, margin, and care.

## Voice

Plainspoken, not simplistic. Warm, not casual. Witty in precise flashes, not performance comedy. Spiritually serious, not vague. Operationally specific, not inspirational fog. Willing to say the thing: "Most organizations are not governing AI. They are hoping policies will behave like systems."

## Vocabulary

**Use more:** governance architecture · leadership architecture · decision systems · human judgment · trust that survives contact with real work · humane operating systems · rightly ordered technology · care infrastructure · work-life architecture · woman leader · accountability, drift, limits, ownership

**Use less:** AI strategy in isolation · personal brand vibes · innovation theater · productivity hacks · best practices · automation stack · tech transformation · mom hacks · balance as vague aspiration · girlboss/hustle language · guardrails as slogan

## Do-not-say

- Do not lead with "AI consultant" or make consulting the primary identity.
- Do not reduce household work to productivity, motherhood, or life hacks — it is leadership infrastructure at household scale.
- Do not make faith the sales hook; it is the source of conviction and moral clarity (visible in About and selected essays).
- Do not sound like a futurist. Sound like an operator who has seen what happens after the demo.
- Do not sterilize the voice — warmth, dry wit, and practical edge are assets.
- Avoid: unlock potential, leverage AI, future-proof, transformation journey, thought leader.
