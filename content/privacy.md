This site is built to respect your attention and your data. Here is the complete picture.

## Analytics

This site uses [GoatCounter](https://www.goatcounter.com/), a privacy-friendly analytics service. It counts page views without cookies, without tracking you across sites, and without collecting personal information. No advertising trackers, no pixels, no fingerprinting.

## Email

If you subscribe to *Letters from the Desk* or open the Field Guide Vault, your email address (and first name, if you share it) is stored with [Mailchimp](https://mailchimp.com/legal/privacy/), which handles delivery. It is used to send you the letters and guides you asked for — nothing else. It is never sold or shared. Every email includes an unsubscribe link, and unsubscribing works immediately.

## Contact form

Messages sent through the contact form are processed by [Netlify Forms](https://www.netlify.com/privacy/) and delivered to Christa. Your message and contact details are used to reply to you, and for no other purpose.

## Fonts and video

Headline and body fonts are served by Google Fonts, which may log requests as described in [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). Video on the home page is hosted on this site directly — no third-party video platforms.

## Questions

Anything unclear, or want your data removed from the mailing list entirely? Write to [christa@christaburger.com](mailto:christa@christaburger.com) and it will be handled — by a person, promptly.
