This site aims to conform to the [Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA](https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG21/quickref/). Good systems should carry everyone.

## What's in place

- **Semantic structure** — proper landmarks, one heading hierarchy per page, and a skip-to-content link.
- **Keyboard navigation** — every menu, link, and form works without a mouse, with visible focus indicators throughout.
- **Color contrast** — text colors are chosen to meet AA contrast ratios against their backgrounds.
- **Reduced motion** — if your system requests reduced motion, animations are disabled and the home-page video will not autoplay.
- **Readable type** — body text at a comfortable size and line height, in a column narrow enough to follow.
- **Alt text** — images that carry meaning have text descriptions; decorative images are hidden from screen readers.
- **No text trapped in images** — nothing important is communicated only through pictures.

## Known limitations

Some older downloadable PDFs in the Field Guide Vault were built as slide decks and may not be fully screen-reader friendly. If you need any of that material in an accessible format, ask — it will be provided.

## Tell me when it breaks

If any part of this site is hard for you to use, that's a defect, not your problem. Write to [christa@christaburger.com](mailto:christa@christaburger.com) with the page and the difficulty, and it will be fixed as quickly as possible.
